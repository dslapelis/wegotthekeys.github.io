Our Specialties

Wood Cleaning

For the novice, wood can be tricky. Too much pressure and the wood is destroyed, not enough and the bacteria grows back. We will strip the deck of previous stain, mildew and that gray UV damaged layer to expose raw wood underneath, then apply finished layer of stain to get your deck looking brand new.

Concrete Washing

Concrete is quick and easy! Give us the grimiest concrete you have: driveways, sidewalks, brick, siding, etc. we will make it look 20 years younger. Even the most neglected concrete can look brand new again.

Siding Spraying

Vinyl siding and painted structures are fragile. Pressure can create cracks in siding which then turns to mildew and mold growing within - an even bigger problem. Bleach can cause paint to wear thin and strip the outer layer, leaving your paint pale and whitewashed or completely stripped. Like wood, house siding and vinyl can be tricky for newbies. Call the pros to get the job done without creating a bigger problem.

Roof Renewal

PNW Pressure Washing will make quick work of your dirty roof. Get the moss and black algae and mold off your roof, it will look brand new in a day!

Fleet Cleaning

Keep your vehicles clean! Washing your fleet regularly is crucial for maintaining your brand as well as the life of the paint on your vehicle. Oil, exhaust, bugs, grease and dirt can cause long term damage to the exterior of your vehicle.

Commercial Washing

We go to work at any time of day! Let us clean while your customers are gone. We service parking garages, shopping plazas, sidewalks, car lots, entrances, siding and can accommodate other requests as well.
